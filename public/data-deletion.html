Data Deletion Instructions for Count My Beer

Last updated: November 23, 2025

We respect your privacy and your right to control your data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from Count My Beer, please follow the instructions below.

How to Delete Your Data

Step 1: Open the Count My Beer app and sign in to your account.
Step 2: Navigate to your Profile section (tap the Profile icon in the bottom navigation).
Step 3: Scroll down and tap on "Delete Account".
Step 4: Confirm your deletion request. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
⚠️ Important: Deleting your account will permanently remove:
  • Your profile information
  • All beer logs and history
  • Your preferences and settings
  • Any data associated with your account
This action cannot be reversed.

Alternative: Request Manual Deletion

If you're unable to access the app or encounter any issues with the in-app deletion process, you can request manual deletion by contacting us with:

  • Your email address associated with the account
  • Subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Confirmation that you want all your data permanently deleted

What Happens After Deletion

Once your account is deleted:

  • Your data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days
  • You will no longer be able to access your account
  • Any data shared with third-party authentication providers (Google, Facebook) will be removed from our systems, but you may need to revoke app permissions separately from those platforms

Revoking Third-Party Access

If you signed in using Google or Facebook, you should also revoke Count My Beer's access:

  • Google: Visit Google Account Permissions and remove Count My Beer
  • Facebook: Go to Settings & Privacy → Settings → Apps and Websites, and remove Count My Beer

Questions?

If you have any questions about data deletion, please refer to our Privacy Policy or contact us.
